General Student Advising
Student Advising > General Student Advising
The TUM general student advising team supports prospective and current students concerning general study-related issues. Reasons to arrange an advising session include:
- General questions regarding degree courses at TUM
- Information on admission requirements and application documents
- Advice on general (extracurricular) issues
- Information on enrollment, application deadlines and submitting documents
Links relevant for questions related to General Student Advising: https://www.tum.de/en/studies/support-and-advice/support-during-studies/studentadvising, https://www.cit.tum.de/cit/studium/studierende/beratung/
