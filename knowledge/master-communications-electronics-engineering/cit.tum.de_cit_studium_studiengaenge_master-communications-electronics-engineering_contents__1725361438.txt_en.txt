Master's Thesis
Degree Program Contents Master in Communications and Electronics Engineering > Master's Thesis
Students can start the master's thesis if they have passed 63 credits from course work plus the research internship. For the registration of the master's thesis students have to contact the MSCE office.
Professors from CIT (department Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics) are allowed to supervise master's thesis and research internship.
Links relevant for questions related to Master's Thesis: https://www.cit.tum.de/cit/studium/studiengaenge/master-communications-electronics-engineering/contents/
